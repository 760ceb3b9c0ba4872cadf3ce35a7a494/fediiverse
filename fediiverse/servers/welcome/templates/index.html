How it works

fediiverse turns the Miiverse applet on your Nintendo 3DS into a window to the Fediverse.

fediiverse stands between your console and the Fediverse. Your 3DS talks to your fediiverse instance, and your fediiverse instance talks to your Mastodon or Mastodon-compatible instance.

fediiverse is open source!
